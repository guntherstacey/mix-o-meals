1 large mango, julienned
1 red pepper, julienned
1 avocado, diced
1/4 cup diced cashews, roasted and unsalted
1/4 cup diced fresh cilantro
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 tablespoon sesame oil
1 tablespoon maple syrup
1/2 tablespoon tamari (or 1 tablespoon of soy sauce)
1 teaspoon sriracha

In a small bowl, add lime juice, sesame oil, maple syrup, tamari, and sriracha. Using a whisk, stir until combined and oil no longer separates. Set aside.
To a medium bowl, add mango, red pepper, avocado, cashews, and fresh cilantro. Pour the sauce over the mango salad and gently toss using two spoons.
Serve immediately,
